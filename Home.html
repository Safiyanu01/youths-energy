YOUTHS ENERGY ORGANIZATION

Youth Energy Organization is a non profit Humanitarian Relief Organization Whose goals include saving and empowering lives, Actions towards achieving Sustainable Development Goals. The international community has set out 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

  • to end poverty,
  • ensure healthy lives and education for all,
  • protect our planet,
  • and among other Goals, build a global partnership for development.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization which strives to improve the quality of life of people living in African rural communities. We address the needs and necessities of the needy to build healthy, sustainable and productive solutions for them through clean water, nutrition, agriculture and sports. Our mission is rebuilding life of the poors with dignity.
